About the Internship

ELLECTRA 5-Day Internship Program is a practical hands-on training program designed for students interested in Electronics, IoT, PCB Designing, 3D Designing, Embedded Systems, and AI-based Web Development.


This internship focuses on real-time learning with live practical sessions, project building, and industry-oriented training. Students will gain both technical knowledge and practical experience by working with electronic components, sensors, PCB software, soldering tools, cloud platforms, and AI-assisted development tools.
